How it works

No app downloads. No complicated setup. Your customers just scan and collect.

1

Customer scans your QR

Place our QR code at your counter. Customers scan it with their phone camera — no app needed.

2

They collect punches

Each visit earns a punch on their digital card. It fills up automatically — just like paper, but better.

3

They earn rewards

When the card is full, they redeem their reward. Happy customers keep coming back.
